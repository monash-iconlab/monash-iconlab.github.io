About

Monash University ICON Lab

ICON Lab is a research group led by Dr Yihai Fang at Monash University. Our work focuses on construction automation and informatics, construction robotics, digital twin applications for the built environment, and construction safety and human factors. We aim to advance intelligent, human-centred, and industry-relevant technologies that improve productivity, safety, and decision-making across construction and infrastructure systems.
